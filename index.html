About ODA SBS

The main purpose compelled the ODA foundation as disscussed by intellectuals are:

  • There was a water supply problem in Oromia eventhrou there was a full of raw materils input for the providense of water. So ODA aimed at finding the remedy to this problem
  • To conserve plants and other natural resources since ther was high declining rate of forest by that time.
  • To full fill infrastructure such as helth care center, rode construction and some other builing for Oromo people.
